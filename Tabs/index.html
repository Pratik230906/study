London

London is the capital city of the United Kingdom. It is famous for landmarks like the Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and the London Eye. The River Thames flows through the city, adding to its beauty. London is a major center for finance, culture, and education. The city experiences cool winters and mild summers with frequent rain.

Paris

Paris is the capital of France and is often called the “City of Light.” It is world-famous for the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. Paris is known for its art, fashion, and delicious cuisine. The River Seine runs through the heart of the city. Millions of tourists visit Paris every year to experience its romance and culture.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital city of Japan and one of the most modern cities in the world. It is known for its tall skyscrapers, technology, and busy streets. The city combines traditional culture with advanced innovations. Famous places in Tokyo include the Tokyo Tower, Shibuya Crossing, and Senso-ji Temple. Tokyo is also a major global hub for business, entertainment, and fashion.
